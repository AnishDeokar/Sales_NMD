*Sales & Business Performance Dashboard using Power BI*

*Description* :-
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to track sales performance, customer behavior, and product
profitability.
Created impactful data visualizations using KPIs, charts, slicers, and drill-downs for deeper analysis.
Applied DAX functions to calculate dynamic measures such as total sales, profit margins, and customer trends.

*Features and Highlights* :- 
1. Business Problem
The company lacked a centralized view of sales performance, customer behavior, and product profitability. This made it difficult for management to quickly identify underperforming areas, track KPIs, and make data-driven decisions.

2. Goal of the Dashboard
To create an interactive dashboard that provides real-time insights into sales trends, product profitability, and customer behavior, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and optimize business strategies.

3. Walkthrough of Key Visuals 
KPIs Cards: Displayed Total Sales, Profit Margin %, and Customer Count for at-a-glance performance tracking.
Sales Trend Chart: Line chart showing monthly/quarterly sales trends to monitor seasonality and growth.
Product Profitability Analysis: Bar/column chart ranking products by revenue and profit margin.

4. Business Impact & Insights
Enabled leadership to track sales growth and identify top-performing products.
Revealed underperforming regions and products that required strategy changes.
Improved decision-making by providing customer purchase behavior insights.
Supported profitability analysis, helping the business focus on high-margin products.
